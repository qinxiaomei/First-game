Inferiority

In recent years, guangxi county economy has achieved relatively rapid development and achieved great results. But there are some problems in guangxi county economy.

(Two)The basic competitiveness of county economy declined


(Three)County economic development imbalance

(Five)The inadequacy of our financial system and the lack of investment in local finance have resulted in the difficulty of financing the county economy.
