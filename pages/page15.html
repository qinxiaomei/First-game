Developing advantage

"One Belt And One Road"
strategy implementation

First，National strategy covers all. The whole region of guangxi has achieved national strategic coverage, and enjoys the overlapping policies of regional autonomy, the development of the west, the opening of coastal areas along the river and the revitalization of the old revolutionary areas.

Secondly，Location advantage is unique. Along the coast of guangxi, along the border and along the river, lies the intersection of the economic circle of the pearl river delta and the asean economic circle.

In the end，Convenient transportation. Guangxi is the most convenient land and sea passage to asean. It is an important transportation hub of east-west and south-western regions in the Middle East.
